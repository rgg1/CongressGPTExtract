    KATHLEEN G. SEBELIUS, Secretary of Health and Human Services; born 
in Cincinnati, OH, May 15, 1948; education: B.S., Trinity University, 
1970; M.P.A., University of Kansas, 1977; professional: elected Governor 
of Kansas, 2002 and re-elected, 2006; before tenure as Governor, served 
as the Kansas State Insurance Commissioner for 8 years; served as a 
member of the Kansas House of Representatives, 1986-94; married: Gary, a 
federal magistrate judge; children: two sons, Ned and John; nominated by 
President Barack Obama to become the 21st Secretary of Health and Human 
Services, confirmed by the U.S. Senate and sworn in, both on April 28, 
2009.
