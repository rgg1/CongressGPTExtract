    PENNY PRITZKER, Secretary of Commerce; born in Chicago, Illinois, 
1959; education: graduated, Castilleja School, Palo Alto, California, 
1977; A.B., economics, Harvard University, 1981; J.D., M.B.A., Stanford 
University, 1984; professional: founder of Vi (formerly Classic Resident 
by Hyatt), 1987; founder of Pritzker Realty Group, 1991; chairman of 
Superior Bank, 1991-1994; co-founder of The Parking Spot, 1998; 
Executive Chairman of the Board of TransUnion, LLC, 2005-2012; co-
founder of Artemis Real Estate Partners, 2010; founder and CEO of PSP 
Capital Partners, 2012; married: Dr. Bryan Traubert; two children: 
Donald Pritzker Traubert, Rose Pritzker Traubert; nominated by President 
Barack Obama to become the 38th Secretary of Commerce, and was confirmed 
by the U.S. Senate on June 16, 2013.
