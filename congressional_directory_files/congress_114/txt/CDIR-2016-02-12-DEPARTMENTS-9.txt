    SYLVIA M. BURWELL, Secretary of Health and Human Services; born in 
Hinton, West Virginia; education: A.B. from Harvard University and a 
B.A. from Oxford University (Rhodes Scholar); Director of the Office of 
Management and Budget (OMB); President of the Walmart Foundation in 
Bentonville, Arkansas; President of the Global Development Program at 
the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation in Seattle, Washington; Deputy 
Director of OMB; Deputy Chief of Staff to the President; Chief of Staff 
to the Secretary of the Treasury; Staff Director of the National 
Economic Council. Nominated by President Barack Obama to become the 21st 
Secretary of Health and Human Services, confirmed by the U.S. Senate on 
June 9, 2014.
