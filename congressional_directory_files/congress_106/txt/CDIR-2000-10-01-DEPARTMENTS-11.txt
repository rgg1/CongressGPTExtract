    RODNEY E. SLATER, Secretary of Transportation, 13th Secretary in the 
30-year history of the department; born February 23, 1955, grew up in 
Marianna, Arkansas; graduated, Eastern Michigan University; LLB, 
University of Arkansas; prior to becoming Secretary served four years as 
Administrator, Federal Highway Administration; Director of Governmental 
Relations, Arkansas State University; executive assistant, Economic and 
Community Programs, and special assistant for Community and Minority 
Affairs for then Arkansas Governor Bill Clinton; Assistant Attorney 
General-Litigation Division of Arkansas State Attorney General's Office; 
liaison, Martin Luther King Jr. Federal Holiday Commission; member: 
Arkansas State Highway Commission, 1987-92, chairman, 1992; married to 
Cassandra Wilkins; one daughter: Bridgette Josette; named Secretary of 
Transportation on February 14, 1997.
