        DELEGATES, REPRESENTATIVES, AND SENATORS SERVING IN THE 
                          1st-106th CONGRESSES \1\
As of June 15, 1999, 11,590 individuals have served: 9,741 only in the House of Representatives, 1,225 only in the Senate, and 624 in both Houses. Total
  serving in the House of Representatives (including individuals serving in both bodies) is 10,365. Total for Senate (including individuals serving in
                                                                both bodies) is 1,849.\2\
