    BRUCE BABBITT, Secretary of the Interior, sworn in on January 22, 
1993, as the 47th Secretary; born June 27, 1938; B.A., Notre Dame, 
student body president; M.S., University of Newcastle in England, 
Marshall Scholar; J.D., Harvard; Attorney General of Arizona, 1975-78; 
Governor of Arizona, 1978-87; chairman, Democratic Governors 
Association, 1985; candidate for Democratic nomination for President, 
1988; married to the former Hattie Coons; two children: Christopher and 
T.J.
