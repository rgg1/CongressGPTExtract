15th and Pennsylvania NW., 20220, phone 622-2000, http://www.ustreas.gov

    PAUL O'NEILL, Secretary of the Treasury; born on December 4, 1935, 
in St. Louis, MO; education: B.A., Fresno State College; M.P.A., Indiana 
University; professional: International Paper Co., 1977-1987, serving as 
President, 1985-1987; and Alcoa, Inc., Chairman and Chief Executive 
Officer, 1987-2000; public service: Veterans Administration, computer 
systems analyst, 1961-1966; Office of Management and Budget, 1967-2977; 
serving as Deputy Director, 1974-1977; family: married to Nancy; four 
children; nominated by President George W. Bush to become the 72nd 
Secretary of the Treasury, and was confirmed by the U.S. Senate on 
January 20, 2001.
