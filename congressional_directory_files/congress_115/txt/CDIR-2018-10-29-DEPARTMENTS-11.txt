University, 1990; J.D., Harvard Law School, 1994; professional: Law

and Public Policy Center, 1998-2000; attorney, Kirkland & Ellis, 1995-

University College of Law, 2009-17; chairman, U.S. Century Bank, 2013-
17; married: Jan Elizabeth Acosta; nominated by President Donald J. 
Trump to become the 27th Secretary of Labor, and was confirmed by the 
U.S. Senate on April 27, 2017.
