    ELAINE L. CHAO, Secretary of Transportation; born in Taipei, Taiwan, 
March 26; education: MBA, Harvard Business School; B.A., Mount Holyoke 
College, South Hadley, MA; professional: Distinguished Fellow at The 
Heritage Foundation, 2009-17 and 1996-2001; U.S. Secretary of Labor, 
2001-09; president and CEO of the United Way of America, 1992-96; 
director of the Peace Corps, 1991-92; Deputy Secretary of 
Transportation, 1989-91; chair of the Federal Maritime Commission, 1988-
89; Deputy Administrator, Maritime Administration, 1986-88; family: 
married to Mitch McConnell, U.S. Senator (KY); nominated by President 
Donald J. Trump to become the 18th Secretary of Transportation, and was 
confirmed by the U.S. Senate on January 31, 2017.
