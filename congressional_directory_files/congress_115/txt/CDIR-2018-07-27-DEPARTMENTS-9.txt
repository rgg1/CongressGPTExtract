    JEFFERSON B. SESSIONS III, Attorney General; born in Selma, AL; 
education: Huntingdon College, 1969; University of Alabama Law School, 
1973; professional: Assistant U.S. Attorney, Southern District of 
Alabama, 1975-79; U.S. Attorney for the Southern District of Alabama, 
1981-93, Attorney General of Alabama, 1995-97; U.S. Senator from 
Alabama, 1997-2017; sworn in as the 84th Attorney General of the United 
States on February 9, 2017 by Michael R. Pence. President Donald J. 
Trump announced his intention to nominate Mr. Sessions on November 18, 
2016.
