     XAVIER BECERRA, Secretary of the Department of Health and Human 
Services and the first Latino to hold the office in the history of the 
United States; born in Sacramento, CA; education: Bachelor of Arts in 
Economics, Stanford University, Palo Alto, CA; Juris Doctorate, Stanford

Means; Ranking Member of the Ways and Means Subcommittee on Social 
Security and Ranking Member of the Subcommittee on Health; Attorney 
General, State of California; married to Dr. Carolina Reyes; children: 
Clarisa, Olivia and Natalia, and son-in-law Ivan.
