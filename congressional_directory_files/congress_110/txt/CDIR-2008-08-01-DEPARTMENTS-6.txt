    MIKE JOHANNS, Secretary of Agriculture; born in Osage, IA; 
education: B.A., St. Mary's University, Minnesota; J.D., Creighton 
University; professional: Governor, Nebraska, 1999-2005; Mayor, Lincoln, 
Nebraska, 1991-98; Lancaster County Board, 1982-88; Lincoln City 
Council, 1989-90; Practicing attorney, 1975-91; member: National 
Governors' Association; Western Governors' Association; married: 
Stephanie; two children; nominated by President George W. Bush to become 
the 28th Secretary of Agriculture, and was confirmed by the U.S. Senate 
on January 20, 2005.
