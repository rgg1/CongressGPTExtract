    MICHAEL O. LEAVITT, Secretary of Health and Human Services; born in 
Cedar City, UT, February 11, 1951; education: B.S., Southern Utah 
University, 1978; professional: served as Administrator of the U.S. 
Environmental Protection Agency; Governor of Utah for 11 years; 
organizations: chairman, National Governors Association; Western 
Governors Association; Republican Governors Association; married: 
Jacalyn; five children; nominated by President George W. Bush to become 
the 20th Secretary of Health and Human Services, and was confirmed by 
the U.S. Senate on January 26, 2005.
