                                    DELEGATES, REPRESENTATIVES, AND SENATORS SERVING IN THE 1st-108th CONGRESSES \1\
As of June 12, 2003, 11,708 individuals have served: 9,833 only in the House of Representatives, 1,240 only in the Senate, and 635 in both Houses. Total
serving in the House of Representatives (including individuals serving in both bodies) is 10,468. Total for the Senate (including individuals serving in
                                                                both bodies) is 1,875.\2\
