                                    DELEGATES, REPRESENTATIVES, AND SENATORS SERVING IN THE 1st-111th CONGRESSES \1\
As of July 16, 2009, 11,897 individuals have served: 9,986 only in the House of Representatives, 1,262 only in the Senate, and 649 in both Houses. Total
serving in the House of Representatives (including individuals serving in both bodies) is 10,635. Total for the Senate (including individuals serving in
                                                                both bodies) is 1,911.\2\
